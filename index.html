About The Singing People

We’re not a choir. We don’t have rehearsals and none of us is any good at reading music. But we do love to sing and chat and drink tea. Sometimes we go out dancing.

We’ve been singing and dancing and chatting and drinking tea since 2011, when a few of us thought it might be fun to start a choir. We soon realised that it was more fun just to turn up and sing and dance and drink tea whenever we felt like it.

We also love meeting new people, so click the button below to join in and tell us how to take your tea.
