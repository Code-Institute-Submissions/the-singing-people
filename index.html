About The Singing People

We’re not a choir. We don’t have rehearsals and none of us is any good at reading music. But we do love to sing and chat and drink tea. Sometimes we go out dancing.

We’ve been singing and dancing and chatting and drinking tea since 2011, when a few of us thought it might be fun to start a choir. We soon realised that it was more fun just to turn up and sing and dance and drink tea whenever we felt like it.

We also love meeting new people, so click the button below to join in and tell us how to take your tea.

How does it work?

Just turn up! We really do love it if you let us know by filling in the form below (so we know how much tea to buy), but if you're not organised enough, no biggie — neither are we! You don't have to pay a membership fee or commit to showing up every week; you don't have to be able to read music or have had any experience of singing. If you get to the first Thursday of the month and feel like a sing (or maybe a dance) and a chat and a cuppa, then come along!

Below are our meet-up and singing times. Once a month we sing, and every couple of months we go to the pub. Those who feel like it can join us for our Big Charity Sing Thing in early December.
